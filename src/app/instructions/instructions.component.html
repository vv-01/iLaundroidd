Instructions

General Information

  • We reserve the right to refuse washing of any clothes.
  • Anybody who does not hold valid membership of laundromat is not allowed or entertained within the laundromat.
  • No brushing shall be done for handLerchief, socks, towels, undergarments or any other delicate clothes as they would get damaged.
  • Do not try to air out your clothes at your premises after collection. This helps in avoiding moisture collection.
  • If any clothes are not washed satisfactorily, then you may bring it back to the laundromat and have it washed in front of you at no extra cost.
  • TAT shall be 48 hours depending upon load and constant supply of water and electricity.
  • Laundry shall be closed on national holidays and important festivals and events.
  • We DO NOT DO DRY CLEANING.
  • NO CARRY FORWARD OF UNUSED WASHES SHALL BE DONE.
  • Please ensure that you are in sync with validity period and recharge window time periods.

Important Instructions

  • Your card is NOT TRANSFERABLE. Handing over your card to your friends will result in immediate confiscation and cancellation of your laundry membership. Your personal presence is mandatory during deposit/collection of clothes.
  • Please try to bring a mix bag of small, medium and big clothes including undergarments.
  • Please do always ensure that you weigh your load, get your card punched and sign the register each time you come to deposit clothes. Please sign outgoing register before collecting clothes back.
  • Please do ensure that all your clothes including socks are turned right-side out before bringing your clothes for washing.
  • Please do bring your clothes on the alloted day of the week as per the rooster. This will ensure smooth functioning.
  • Please do ensure you take proper care of your card and bag and don't lose/damage either of them. A fine of Rs. 100/- each shall be charged for new card or bag.
  • Please do immediately report loss of card at the laundromat and get your card number blocked.

Additional Instructions

  • Please DO NOT BRING COLOR-BLEEDING CLOTHES for washing to the laundromat. This will result in your clothes getting spoilt for which laundry is not responsible. Do not gice clothes that require dry-cleaning only or hand-wash only.
  • Please DO NOT bring more than 1 bedsheet, 2 towels, 4 jeans in a given load of around 6KG. This results in inferior wash quality and this must be avoided.
  • Please DO NOT bring any other clothes when giving single-bed blankets. One blanket will be treated as one wash.
  • Please DO NOT leave valuables in pockets of your clothes. We are not responsible for any valuables.
  • Please DO NOT leave anything like chewing gum, gutka, chalk, tablets, ink, pen in your pockets; it may result in stained clothes.
  • Please DO NOT pick up anybody else's bag or clothes within the laundry.
  • Please DO NOT give woolen clothes along with normal clothes. Give it as a seperate load.
